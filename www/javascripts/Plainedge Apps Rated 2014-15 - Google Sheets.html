COUNTIFS(criteria_range1, criterion1, [criteria_range2, criterion2, ...])
Example
COUNTIFS(A1:A10, ">20", B1:B10, "<30")
Summary
Returns the count of a range depending on multiple criteria.
criteria_range1
The range to check against criterion1.
criterion1
The pattern or test to apply to criteria_range1.
criteria_range2, criterion2... - [optional] repeatable
Additional ranges and criteria to check.
